About This Product

Our Heritage Grain Sourdough represents three generations of artisan baking tradition, crafted with ancient grains and a 150-year-old sourdough starter that creates the perfect balance of tangy complexity and hearty satisfaction.

Each loaf is hand-shaped and slow-fermented for 24 hours, developing deep flavors and improved digestibility. The combination of heritage wheat, spelt, and rye creates a robust texture with a golden, crackling crust that gives way to an airy, hole-filled crumb.

Perfect for morning toast with our wildflower honey, afternoon sandwiches, or evening bread service alongside artisan cheeses and charcuterie.
